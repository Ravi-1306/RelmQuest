Community Rules

    Fair Play and Respect for Rules

    Do not cheat, exploit game mechanics, or engage in disruptive behavior. Report any rule violations you encounter.

    Bullying and Harassment

    Treat all members with kindness and respect. Do not engage in bullying, harassment, or any behavior intended to harm or intimidate others. Always maintain a positive and friendly atmosphere.

    Inclusivity and Tolerance

    Our roleplaying community is open to individuals of all backgrounds and identities. Do not engage in any form of discrimination or hate speech based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, ability, or any other characteristic.

    No Erotic Roleplaying

    We are committed to maintaining a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members and of all ages. To ensure that our community remains a safe and comfortable space for everyone, the rule is clear.

    Respect for Privacy

    Treat all members with kindness and respect. Do not engage in bullying, harassment, or any behavior intended to harm or intimidate others. Always maintain a positive and friendly atmosphere.

    Impersonation

    Do not impersonate other members or any fictional or real-world personalities. Taking credit for someone else's creations or deceiving others is not allowed.

    Legal and Safe Activities

    Do not promote, participate in, or endorse illegal or dangerous activities, including but not limited to illegal drug use, gambling, harassment, doxing, or sharing violent content. Any threats, even as jokes, are taken seriously.

    Scams and Deception

    Avoid scams and deceptive practices. Do not seek or share account information, engage in fraudulent trades, or buy/sell personal information.

    Content Guidelines

    Ensure that all content, including roleplay posts, follows the forum's content guidelines. Avoid any content that may disrupt the positive experiences of other members.

    Player Reporting

    If you encounter a member who violates the community rules or disrupts the experience of others, report them to the forum moderators using the designated reporting system. Blocking is also an option for personal comfort.

    Account Security

    Protect your account information and avoid sharing it with others. Suspicious or too-good-to-be-true offers should be treated with caution. Do not share your account or the accounts of others.

    Safety Measures

    Familiarize yourself with the safety measures provided by the forum. These measures are designed to keep the community safe and secure. Take advantage of any tools available for account protection and online safety.
